Spellify: A website to study spelling online.


Enter a custom word bank.

On the main page, enter your spelling words (seperated by a comma and a space) and press start.


Save the spelling words for later.

Once you enter your spelling words and press start, the words will be stored on your device for later use.


Test your knowledge.

Press the audio button and practice your spelling skills. Then press submit to see how you did.


See your improvements.

This website can help you efficently study spelling for a better grade on your next quiz or test.
